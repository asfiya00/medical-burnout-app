EXERCISES

Breathing & Relaxation

  • Inhale for 4 seconds
  • Exhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds
  • Repeat for 1–4 minutes

Mindfulness & Meditation

  • 5-Minute Meditation: Focus on breath
  • Body Scan: Relax each muscle

Visualization

  • Imagine a peaceful scene
  • Engage senses: sounds, smells
  • Breathe deeply for 5–10 min

Relaxation Exercise

  • Tense muscles for 5 sec, release
  • Focus on full body relaxation

Stretching & Mobility

  • Neck Rolls: Clockwise + counter
  • Shoulder Shrugs: Hold for 3 sec

Yoga – Child’s Pose

  • Sit on heels, extend arms
  • Forehead on floor, breathe

Laughter Therapy

  • Fake smile & laugh
  • Watch funny videos
  • Recall happy memories

Quick Physical Activity

  • 5-min Cardio: Jumping jacks, jog
  • Chair Squats: Up & down 1 min
