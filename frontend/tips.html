Effective Ways to Relieve Stress

1. Mindfulness or Meditation

Sit in a quiet space, close your eyes, and focus on your breath.
Start with 5 minutes daily to train your mind to stay calm and focused.

2. Health Sleep Habits

Prioritize 7–9 hours of good sleep.
Avoid phones, laptops, and TVs at least 30–60 minutes before sleeping.

3. Engage in Hobbies

Make time for what you love like reading books, painting, music, gardening, etc.
Sharing your feelings with a friend, therapist, or support group can reduce the emotional burden.

4. Quick Activities

Go for a short walk.
Drink a glass of water mindfully.
Listen to your favourite music.

5. Take Break

Schedule regular breaks throughout the day to avoid burnout and allow yourself time to relax and recharge.
Breathe, pause, and give yourself permission to rest.

6. Exercise Regularly

Even 20–30 minutes of activity a few times a week boosts endorphins and reduces stress.
