Recommended Games to De-Stress

Boost your mood and refresh your mind with these calming and fun games.

1. Bubble Wrap Popper

  • A simple clickable pop game.
  • Designed to calm your mind with simple, satisfying pops.

2. Memory Card Game

  • Activates the brain in a low-pressure way.
  • Improves concentration.
  • Boosts mood by completing small tasks successfully.

3. Fun Board Games

  • Like Snakes and Ladders, Ludo, etc.
  • Helps medical professionals turn stress relief into a daily habit.

4. Puzzles

  • Improves focus, patience, and visual-spatial skills.
  • Great for solo or group play.

5. Candy Crush

  • Popular free-to-play match-three puzzle.
  • Serves as a positive and enjoyable stress reliever.

6. Calm Jenga

  • A physical skill game requiring steady hands and concentration.
  • Promotes mindfulness and diverts attention from stressors.
